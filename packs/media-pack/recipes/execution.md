# Media Pack Execution

## Workflow Order

1. `brief-intake` - Normalize goals, audience, deliverables, references, and policy constraints.
2. `asset-plan` - Choose tools, source assets, shot lists, and production order before execution.
3. `production` - Create the primary media outputs and intermediate artifacts.
4. `quality-gate` - Check technical quality, policy compliance, and packaging before handoff.

## Execution Rules

- Preserve source asset paths, prompts, model or tool versions, and transform steps for every artifact.
- Prefer non-destructive editing and keep a master output before generating delivery-specific variants.
- Separate confirmed facts from inferred creative assumptions in captions, metadata, and handoff notes.
- Fail fast when a deliverable cannot meet requested format, codec, duration, or policy constraints.

## Required Handoff Fields

- brief
- artifact manifest
- source provenance
- tool and transform log
- confirmed facts
- inferred assumptions
- qc results
- remaining risks
- next adaptation opportunities
