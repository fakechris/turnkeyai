# Media Pack Intake

## Inputs

- campaign or product objective
- target channel, audience, and placement
- requested deliverables by modality and format
- source assets, brand references, and prior examples
- rights, policy, and approval constraints
- non-goals and out-of-scope edits

## Checklist

1. Restate the objective and name the requested modalities: image, video, audio, or adaptation-only.
2. Build a deliverable matrix: artifact, channel, format, dimensions or duration, acceptance bar.
3. Inventory source assets as required, optional, or missing; note rights or licensing risk.
4. Decide whether each deliverable is generation, edit-from-source, adaptation, or mixed.
5. Mark important facts as confirmed, inferred, or missing before production starts.
6. Stop if a required source asset, approval, or output contract is missing.

## Output

- normalized media brief
- deliverable matrix
- asset and rights inventory
- production path per artifact
- approval blockers and open questions
